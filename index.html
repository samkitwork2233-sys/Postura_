About Postura

Poor posture affects millions of students, professionals, athletes, and patients every day. Postura is a wearable device designed to encourage healthier posture by detecting slouching in real time and providing gentle vibration feedback. It also tracks posture trends to support long-term improvement.

Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuously detects posture using motion sensors.

Instant Vibration Alerts

Notifies users immediately when poor posture is detected.

Progress Tracking

Monitor posture trends and daily improvement.

Comfortable Design

Lightweight wearable designed for everyday use.
